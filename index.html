Before You Donate

Please read these 4 quick steps first

Givebutter is free for nonprofits — but the platform asks donors for an optional tip. You can decline it. Follow the steps below so 100% of your gift reaches the mosque, then scroll down to the donation form.

Important: By default, Givebutter adds a processing fee and a 15% tip on top of your donation. The steps below show you exactly where to click to remove both, so 100% of your gift reaches the mosque.
1

Choose your donation amount

Tap one of the preset amounts or type your own in the box, then click Continue.

2

Remove the processing fee

On the summary screen, you'll see a processing fee added on top. Click the green "Edit" link next to it, then uncheck the "I want to cover fees" box.

★ Critical step — don't skip.
3

Set the optional tip to zero

Givebutter pre-selects a 15% tip that does not go to the mosque. Open the tip dropdown and choose "Other", then bring the amount down to $0 using the minus button (or type 0).

★ Critical step — don't skip.
4

Choose your payment method & submit

Pick card, bank, Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, or Venmo — Givebutter accepts them all. Then click Donate. You'll receive a receipt by email. Jazak Allahu khairan — may Allah reward you.
